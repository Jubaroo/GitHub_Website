  • Small Printer

    Stores and organizes items efficiently. Increases storage capacity within the base.

    Provides a navigational aid and base marker for explorers and travelers.
  • Solar Panel
    Generates power from sunlight, essential for maintaining base operations and equipment.
  • Oxygen Tank
    Provides breathable air for explorers during extended missions and cave explorations.

Rovers

  • Buggy
    A small, agile vehicle ideal for quick surface travel. It has limited storage and requires a Rover Seat for operation.
  • Tractor
    A compact vehicle capable of towing up to three Trailers, making it useful for early-game resource transport. It includes a built-in seat.
  • Trailer
    An attachable unit that extends the storage capacity of vehicles like the Tractor. It cannot operate independently.
  • Medium Rover
    A balanced vehicle offering moderate speed and storage, suitable for mid-game exploration. Requires a Rover Seat.
  • Large Rover
    The largest land vehicle with substantial storage and the ability to support various modules. Requires a Rover Seat.

Shuttles

  • Small Shuttle
    Enables basic space travel between planets. It has no storage and requires a Thruster and Oxygenator.
  • Medium Shuttle
    Offers greater storage and range for interplanetary journeys. Requires a Thruster and Oxygenator.
  • Large Shuttle
    Provides the most storage and functionality for space exploration. Requires a Thruster and Oxygenator.

Jet-Powered Vehicles

  • Hoverboard
    A personal mobility device that allows for faster travel than by simply running. It is placed in the player's Backpack and activated when needed. It is driven by electrical power. It can't be researched but has to be unlocked via Missions.
  • VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing)
    A lightweight aircraft that allows travel over long distances by flying over the surface of a planet. It uses Hydrazine as fuel and has two Tier 1 and two Tier 2 slots for passengers or cargo. It can't be researched but has to be unlocked via Missions.

Rail Vehicles

  • Rail Cars and Rail Engines
    Components that allow players to create trains to ride along rail lines set down via Rail Post Bundles or Tall Rail Post Bundles.

Other Vehicles

  • Gravity Globe
    A spherical recreational item that players may sit inside and control as with normal vehicles. It doesn't have any slots, meaning nothing can be placed inside except for Galastropods, which can drive Gravity Globes around on their own.
  • LREV (Long Range Exploration Vehicle)
    The spaceship that brings Astroneers to the solar system. The player begins their game at the LREV, launching a drop pod to the planet below.
  • Refinery
    Processes raw materials into usable resources, essential for advanced crafting.
  • Water Recycler
    Converts wastewater into clean water, supporting sustainable base operations.
  • Power Generator
    Provides a stable power source to keep all base equipment and structures operational.
