Astroneer Resources & Items

  • Compound
    Commonly found on all planets; used in crafting basic items like Tethers and Small Generators.
  • Resin
    Abundant on all planets; essential for creating platforms and storage units.
  • Organic
    Collected from flora; used as fuel in Small Generators and for crafting Carbon.
  • Clay
    Mined on all planets; smelted into Ceramic for crafting items like Drill Mods.
  • Quartz
    Available on all planets; smelted into Glass for crafting Solar Panels and Beacons.
  • Graphite
    Found on all planets; used to craft Packagers and in composite materials.
  • Ammonium
    Present on all planets; used to produce Hydrazine fuel.
  • Laterite
    Found on all planets; smelted into Aluminum for vehicles and modules.
  • Malachite
    Located on Sylva and Calidor; smelted into Copper for electrical items.
  • Sphalerite
    Found on Sylva and Desolo; smelted into Zinc for crafting batteries.
  • Wolframite
    Present on Desolo and Calidor; smelted into Tungsten for advanced modules.
  • Hematite
    Located on Novus and Glacio; smelted into Iron for crafting large structures.
  • Lithium
    Found on Vesania and Novus; used in crafting batteries.
  • Titanite
    Present on Glacio and Vesania; smelted into Titanium for high-end equipment.
  • Astronium
    Location and Extraction:
    • Planetary Cores: Astronium is located in the outer core layers of every planet. Players must dig deep toward the planet's center to access it.
    • Surface Deposits: During specific events, such as the F.A.R.M update, Astronium can also be found inside purple Spookysquash.
    Uses:
    • Trading:
      • EXO Chip: 3 Astronium for 1 EXO Chip.
      • Packager: 1 Astronium for 4 Packagers.
      • Solid-Fuel Jump Jet: 1 Astronium for 2 Jump Jets.
      • Dynamite: 3 Astronium for 4 Dynamite.
      • Hydrazine: 3 Astronium for 4 Hydrazine.
      • Portable Smelting Furnace: 8 Astronium for 1 Furnace.
      • Gravity Globe: 8 Astronium for 1 Globe.
    • Research: Placing Astronium in a Research Chamber yields 500 bytes over 2.5 minutes, equating to 200 bytes per minute per nugget.
    • EXO Dynamics Research Aids: Some research aids require Astronium to open, rewarding players with items like the QT-RTG.
    Additional Information:
    • Appearance: Astronium deposits are iridescent clusters, fading from green in the center to fuchsia on the edge.
    • Historical Changes: Before Patch 1.0, Astronium deposits were bright fuchsia. The nuggets' color has changed over time, reflecting various updates.
    ASTRONEER WIKI
  • Aluminum
    Smelted from Laterite; used in crafting vehicles and modules.
  • Copper
    Smelted from Malachite; essential for electrical components.
  • Zinc
    Smelted from Sphalerite; used in crafting batteries and sensors.
  • Tungsten
    Smelted from Wolframite; necessary for advanced modules like the Chemistry Lab.
  • Iron
    Smelted from Hematite; used in large structures and equipment.
  • Titanium
    Smelted from Titanite; used in high-end tools and vehicles.
  • Ceramic
    Smelted from Clay; used in crafting Drill Mods and other items.
  • Glass
    Smelted from Quartz; used in crafting Solar Panels and Beacons.
  • Carbon
    Smelted from Organic; used in crafting composite materials and as fuel.
  • Hydrogen
    Collected on Vesania (100 ppu), Sylva (75 ppu), Calidor (50 ppu), and Novus (25 ppu); used in crafting Hydrazine.
  • Nitrogen
    Available on Sylva (100 ppu), Vesania (75 ppu), and Atrox (50 ppu); used in composite materials.
  • Argon
    Found on Glacio (100 ppu) and Vesania (50 ppu); used in crafting Steel.
  • Methane
    Collected on Atrox (100 ppu) and Novus (75 ppu); used in crafting Silicone.
  • Sulfur
    Available on Calidor (100 ppu) and Atrox (75 ppu); used in crafting Explosive Powder.
  • Helium
    Found on Atrox (25 ppu); used in crafting Nanocarbon Alloy.
  • Rubber
    Made from Organic and Resin.
  • Plastic
    Made from Carbon and Compound.
  • Aluminum Alloy
    Made from Aluminum and Copper.
  • Tungsten Carbide
    Made from Tungsten and Carbon.
  • Graphene
    Made from Graphite and Hydrazine.
  • Diamond
    Made from two Graphene.
  • Hydrazine
    Made from two Ammonium and Hydrogen.
  • Silicone
    Made from Resin, Quartz, and Methane.
  • Explosive Powder
    Made from two Carbon and Sulfur.
  • Steel
    Made from Iron, Carbon, and Argon.
  • Titanium Alloy
    Made from Titanium, Graphene, and Nitrogen.
  • Nanocarbon Alloy
    Made from Titanium Alloy, Steel, and Helium.
  • Small Printer
    Used to print basic items and structures. Essential for early base development.
  • Large Printer
    Allows for larger constructions and more complex item printing. Supports advanced base infrastructure.
  • Packager
    Stores and organizes items efficiently. Increases storage capacity within the base.
  • Beacon
    Provides a navigational aid and base marker for explorers and travelers.
  • Solar Panel
    Generates power from sunlight, essential for maintaining base operations and equipment.
  • Oxygen Tank
    Provides breathable air for explorers during extended missions and cave explorations.

Rovers

  • Buggy
    A small, agile vehicle ideal for quick surface travel. It has limited storage and requires a Rover Seat for operation.
  • Tractor
    A compact vehicle capable of towing up to three Trailers, making it useful for early-game resource transport. It includes a built-in seat.
  • Trailer
    An attachable unit that extends the storage capacity of vehicles like the Tractor. It cannot operate independently.
  • Medium Rover
    A balanced vehicle offering moderate speed and storage, suitable for mid-game exploration. Requires a Rover Seat.
  • Large Rover
    The largest land vehicle with substantial storage and the ability to support various modules. Requires a Rover Seat.

Shuttles

  • Small Shuttle
    Enables basic space travel between planets. It has no storage and requires a Thruster and Oxygenator.
  • Medium Shuttle
    Offers greater storage and range for interplanetary journeys. Requires a Thruster and Oxygenator.
  • Large Shuttle
    Provides the most storage and functionality for space exploration. Requires a Thruster and Oxygenator.

Jet-Powered Vehicles

  • Hoverboard
    A personal mobility device that allows for faster travel than by simply running. It is placed in the player's Backpack and activated when needed. It is driven by electrical power. It can't be researched but has to be unlocked via Missions.
  • VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing)
    A lightweight aircraft that allows travel over long distances by flying over the surface of a planet. It uses Hydrazine as fuel and has two Tier 1 and two Tier 2 slots for passengers or cargo. It can't be researched but has to be unlocked via Missions.

Rail Vehicles

  • Rail Cars and Rail Engines
    Components that allow players to create trains to ride along rail lines set down via Rail Post Bundles or Tall Rail Post Bundles.

Other Vehicles

  • Gravity Globe
    A spherical recreational item that players may sit inside and control as with normal vehicles. It doesn't have any slots, meaning nothing can be placed inside except for Galastropods, which can drive Gravity Globes around on their own.
  • LREV (Long Range Exploration Vehicle)
    The spaceship that brings Astroneers to the solar system. The player begins their game at the LREV, launching a drop pod to the planet below.
  • Refinery
    Processes raw materials into usable resources, essential for advanced crafting.
  • Water Recycler
    Converts wastewater into clean water, supporting sustainable base operations.
  • Power Generator
    Provides a stable power source to keep all base equipment and structures operational.
